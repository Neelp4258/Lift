Mobile App User Manual

Welcome to the Team

This guide explains how to use the Ambivare Solutions management app on your mobile device. Use the sections below to learn about each feature.

üöÄ Getting Started

Logging In & Out

You must log in to use the system. Your username and password will be provided by your administrator.

  • Login: Open the app and enter your Username and Password on the login screen. Tap "Sign In".
  • Logout: From the main app screen, tap the Logout button in the top-right corner.

Main Screen

After logging in, you'll see the main screen. The top bar shows your name and role. The navigation tabs (e.g., Dashboard, Warehouse, Projects) are at the top of the content area. Tap any tab to switch to that module.

Offline Mode: If you see an "OFFLINE MODE" badge, your device is not connected to the internet. Your changes will be saved locally and sync automatically when you go back online.
üè† The Dashboard

The Dashboard is the first screen you see. It gives you a quick overview of the entire business.

  • Stats Cards: These show key numbers like Total Items, Active Projects, and Maintenance Due.
  • Low Stock Alert: A warning will appear at the top if any items in the inventory are running low.
  • Charts: Visual charts show you the breakdown of inventory by location (Warehouse, Office, Van) and the overall stock levels.
üì¶ Inventory Management

The Warehouse, Office, and Service Van tabs all work the same way. They help you track all company parts and supplies.

Key Inventory Tasks

  • Add a New Item: Tap the + Add Item button. Fill out the form with the item's name, category, quantity, etc.
  • Add More Stock: Find the item in the list and tap the + Add button. Enter the quantity you are adding to the existing stock.
  • Use Stock for a Project: Tap the Use button. Enter the quantity you are taking and select the project this stock is for. This automatically updates the project's records.

Finding Items

Use the Search bar and Filter dropdowns at the top of the list to quickly find the item you need by name, category, or stock status (e.g., "Low Stock").

üì∑ Barcode Scanning (Mobile Only)

Tap the Scan Barcode button to use your phone's camera.

  • If the barcode matches an item, it will automatically open that item's details.
  • If the barcode is not found, it will open the "Add Item" form and pre-fill the barcode number for you.
üèóÔ∏è Project Management

The Projects tab tracks all client installations and modernizations from start to finish.

  • Create a Project: Tap + Create Project to open the form. You must fill in the Project Name, Code, Client, and elevator details.
  • View Projects: The main list shows all projects. You can see their status (Active, Completed) and progress percentage.
  • Edit a Project: Tap the "Edit" button to update a project's status, progress, or other details.
On Hold Projects: If a project is stalled (e.g., waiting for client payment, site not ready), edit the project, change its Status to "On Hold," and fill in the Hold Information section. This is crucial for reports.
üîß Maintenance (Technicians)

This module tracks all service calls, complaints, and scheduled maintenance for client elevators.

Scheduling a Service Call

Tap the + Schedule Maintenance button.

  1. Select the Project and the specific Elevator(s).
  2. Fill in the Issue Description.
  3. Enter the Complaint Person Details (name, phone, address). This is required.
  4. Set a Priority (High, Medium, Low) and Assign it to a technician.
  5. Set the Due Date.

The Service Workflow (Important!)

When you edit a maintenance task, you must update its Status. This is how the office tracks your work.

  • In Progress: When you arrive at the site, change the status to "In Progress". The app will ask for the Arrival Signature from the building representative to confirm you are there.
  • Completed: When the job is finished, change the status to "Completed". You must fill in the Work Performed details and get a Completion Signature from the client.
  • On Hold: If you cannot complete the job (e.g., parts needed), set the status to "On Hold" and fill in the reason.
Exporting Reports: After a job is "Completed" and signed, you can tap the "Edit" button and then the Export PDF button to generate a signed service report for the client.
üéØ Sales (Lead Management)

The Sales tab is for the sales team to manage new leads and potential customers.

Adding & Managing Leads

  • Add a Lead: Tap + Add Lead. Fill in all the details you have (Contact, Company, Project Requirements, Budget, etc.).
  • Update a Lead: Tap "Edit" on any lead to update its information, change its status (e.g., "New" to "Contacted"), or adjust its priority (Hot, Warm, Cold).

Logging Activities (Crucial!)

Tracking your interactions is the most important part of sales.

  1. Tap the View button on a lead.
  2. In the details pop-up, tap the + Add Activity button.
  3. Select the Activity Type (Call, Email, Meeting, Site Visit).
  4. Fill in what was discussed and what the Next Step is (e.g., "Follow-up Call" on a specific date).

This creates a complete timeline for each lead so you never forget to follow up.

üìÑ Billing & Invoicing

The Billing tab allows you to create professional invoices and quotations (estimates).

  • Create Invoice/Quotation: Tap + Create Invoice or + Create Quotation.
  • Fill Details: Add the client's information, then add line items (description, quantity, rate). The app calculates the subtotal, GST, and total amount for you.
  • Payment Terms: You can set different payment terms:
    • Advance + Balance: Lets you record an advance payment and shows the remaining balance due.
    • Multiple Installments: Lets you set up a payment schedule with specific dates and amounts.
  • Generate PDF: Once an invoice is saved, you can tap the PDF button in the list to download a professional PDF to send to the client.
